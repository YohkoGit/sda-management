Direct translation of the existing mock app prototype

+ Proven identity+ Consistent with mock+ Clear hierarchy
~ Public layer may feel too similar to authenticated

Direction B: Warm Community

Church-first warmth with rounded hero and cream tones

The public layer feels like a warm church bulletin — cream background, rounded hero corners, emoji accents, pill-shaped login button. The authenticated layer uses a deep indigo sidebar contrasting with the warm content area. Prioritizes emotional belonging over operational efficiency.

+ Most welcoming public layer+ Strong community feel+ Clear register shift
~ Cream tones may feel dated~ Less "premium/modern"

Direction C: Modern Minimal

Notion-inspired typography-first design — maximum whitespace

No dark hero. Typography does all the heavy lifting. Content is separated by subtle lines instead of cards. The public layer is clean and scannable like a well-typeset document. The authenticated layer uses a quiet sidebar with no color accents — just weight differences. Radical simplicity.

+ Cleanest reading experience+ Timeless design+ Fast to scan
~ May lack distinctive identity~ No visual drama~ Loses mock's premium feel

Direction D: Bold Dashboard

Full dark mode public layer — maximum operational gravity

The entire app lives in dark mode — public AND authenticated. Dense data rows replace spacious cards on the public layer. Department colors appear as left-border accents. The feel is a mission control terminal. Bold choice — distinctive but may feel cold for the church community.

+ Most distinctive+ Strong brand identity+ Data-dense
~ Public layer may feel unwelcoming~ Less accessible for older users~ No warm/cold register shift

Direction E: Gradient Glow

Indigo-to-violet gradient hero — modern web trend, premium feel

The public hero uses a vivid indigo-to-violet gradient with a curved bottom edge. Cards have subtle indigo shadow glow. The authenticated layer stays clean on white/slate-50 but uses gradient accents in the sidebar. Feels modern and polished — a step beyond the mock's flat indigo.

+ Most visually striking+ Modern premium feel+ Strong hero impact
~ Gradient may feel trendy/dated later~ Violet shift diverges from mock's pure indigo

Direction F: Split Personality

Maximum contrast between warm public and dark operational

The public layer is light, warm, and church-bulletin styled — white hero with a bold indigo bottom border, structured but friendly. The authenticated layer is the darkest, most operational interpretation — near-black background, indigo glow effects, uppercase everything, military command terminal feel. The biggest register gap of all directions.

+ Strongest dual-register identity+ Public feels approachable+ Operational feels powerful
~ Transition may feel jarring~ Two very different aesthetics to maintain
